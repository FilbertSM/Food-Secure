About Us

We are a diverse team of college students from various academic backgrounds, united by our commitment to a sustainable future. Our innovative process transforms organic waste using maggots into a 2-in-1, ready-to-use growing medium and fertilizer, empowering communities in high-stunting areas to cultivate nutrient-dense food right in their own backyards. By maintaining our system and collaborating with NGOs, local communities, and government, we support local farmers and nurture a resilient, interconnected food ecosystem.
