Our Program

We transform organic waste using maggots into a 2-in-1, ready-to-use soil medium and fertilizer. Our process combines processed maggot compost with coconut coir waste and essential nutrients.

This innovative solution kickstarts urban farming training in high-stunting areas, enabling local communities to cultivate nutrient-dense food in their home gardens while also commercializing the product to support local farmers and sustain the system.
